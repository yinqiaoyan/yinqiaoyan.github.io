I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Mathematics, Statistics and Mechanics, Beijing University of Technology (BJUT). I am also a Postdoctoral Scholar working with [Prof. Zhongzhan Zhang](https://msm.bjut.edu.cn/info/1094/2658.htm). I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from the Institute of Statistics and Big Data, Renmin University of China (RUC) in 2024 under the supervision of [Prof. Xiangyu Luo](http://isbd.ruc.edu.cn/sztd/a3612cf52e7c4df2bf9c6a403d12b635.htm). Prior to that, I completed my B.Sc. in Statistics at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2019 under the supervision of [Prof. Qingna Li](https://math.bit.edu.cn/szdw/jgml/sxgcx/lqn/index.htm).

My research interests focus on Bayesian statistics, nonparametric Bayes, bioinformatics, spatial transcriptomics, multi-omics integration, and statistical computing.
